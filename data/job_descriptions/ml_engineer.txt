Machine Learning Engineer
AI Ventures | Hyderabad, India | Full-time

Required Qualifications:

- Hands-on experience with machine learning frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch, or scikit-learn

- Familiarity with ML experiment tracking (MLflow, Weights & Biases)
- Experience deploying models using REST APIs (FastAPI or Flask)

- Ability to communicate complex results clearly to non-technical stakeholders
