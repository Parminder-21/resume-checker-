Data Analyst
FinEdge Analytics | Mumbai, India | Full-time

About the Role:
We are hiring a Data Analyst to work with our business intelligence team. You will turn raw data into actionable insights that drive product and business decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets to identify trends, patterns, and business opportunities
- Build and maintain dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Write SQL queries to extract and transform data from multiple sources
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams on data requirements
- Perform A/B testing analysis and communicate results clearly
- Automate recurring reporting workflows using Python

Required Qualifications:
- 1–3 years of experience in data analysis or a related field
- Strong proficiency in SQL (complex joins, window functions, CTEs)
- Experience with Python for data analysis (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib)
- Experience with a BI tool (Tableau, Power BI, or Looker)
- Excellent data visualization and storytelling skills
- Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving ability
- Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field

Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience with dbt or similar data transformation tools
- Familiarity with Apache Spark or big data tools
- Knowledge of statistical analysis and hypothesis testing
- Experience with Google BigQuery or Snowflake
- Exposure to machine learning concepts
- Experience working in Agile/Scrum teams

Nice to Have:
- Excel (advanced) and Google Sheets expertise
- Familiarity with ETL pipelines
- Experience with Airflow or workflow orchestration tools
